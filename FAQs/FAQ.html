LIC (Life Insurance Corporation of India) is the largest insurance provider in India, offering a wide range of reliable and customizable insurance products designed to meet various financial needs. Choosing LIC ensures financial security and peace of mind for you and your family.

You can easily get in touch with a licensed LIC agent by visiting our website's "Contact Us" page, where you'll find options to fill out a form or call/email directly. Our agents are ready to assist you with personalized insurance solutions.

LIC offers diverse insurance plans including term insurance, endowment plans, whole life policies, money-back plans, pension plans, and health insurance. The best policy for you depends on factors like your age, financial goals, and risk appetite. Our agents can guide you in choosing the most suitable option.

Purchasing an LIC policy is hassle-free. You can contact an LIC agent who will assist you throughout the process. Basic documents like identity proof, address proof, and income proof may be required along with the filled application form.

LIC policies offer a unique blend of insurance protection and wealth accumulation. They provide financial security to your loved ones in case of unforeseen events while also offering long-term savings and investment opportunities with tax benefits.

Premiums for LIC policies are calculated based on factors like age, sum assured, policy term, and additional riders chosen. You can easily estimate your premium using our online premium calculator available on the website.

Yes, LIC provides convenient online premium payment options through our website. You can pay securely using net banking, debit/credit cards, or various mobile payment apps.

In the unfortunate event of a claim, our dedicated team ensures a hassle-free settlement process. You need to inform us about the claim, submit required documents, and our team will guide you through the rest of the process swiftly and efficiently.

Yes, LIC policies offer attractive tax benefits under sections 80C and 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act. Premiums paid towards LIC policies are eligible for tax deductions, and the maturity/death benefits are tax-exempt.

You can easily check the status of your LIC policy online through our website's customer portal. Simply log in with your policy details, and you can access information such as premium due dates, policy status, and benefits.

Yes, you can surrender your LIC policy before maturity if required. However, surrendering a policy may lead to loss of insurance cover and accumulated bonuses. Our agents can help you understand the implications and explore alternative options.

LIC offers a grace period for premium payment, typically 30 days. If you miss paying the premium within this period, the policy may lapse, but you can reinstate it within a specified period by paying the due premiums and applicable charges.

Yes, LIC policies offer loan facilities where you can borrow against the surrender value of your policy. The loan amount and terms vary depending on the policy type and duration.

Yes, you can update the nominee for your LIC policy at any time during the policy term by submitting a duly filled nomination form to the nearest LIC branch office.

Yes, LIC offers dedicated plans to secure your child's education and plan for retirement. These plans are designed to provide financial stability and ensure that you can fulfill your responsibilities and dreams without compromise.
